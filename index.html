Frequently Asked Questions

What is Doozyjo? +

Doozyjo is a next-generation crowdfunding platform designed to connect innovative creators with a passionate community of backers. We focus on technology, design, and art projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.

How is it different from other platforms? +

We differentiate ourselves through a curated selection of high-quality projects, a more engaging and interactive community experience, and transparent fees. Our goal is to foster long-term relationships between creators and backers.

When is the official launch? +

We are targeting a launch in Q4 2024. By signing up for our waitlist, you'll be the first to receive updates, exclusive sneak peeks, and an invitation to our early access program.

Is it free to sign up? +

Yes, absolutely! Signing up for the waitlist is completely free and comes with no obligations. It simply ensures you get notified and have the opportunity for early access.
